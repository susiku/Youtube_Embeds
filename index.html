How to Cook Perfect Beef Stir Fry Every Time

Chinese meat dish

Savoury taste

Tantalising soupe

ingredients

  • Oyster sauce, green onions,lemon
  • black pepper, an egg, corn starch, cooking oil
  • Carrots, broccoli, garlic
  • Ginger

Preparation Steps

  1. slice meat into small pieces and put it into a bowl
  2. Add broccoli and egg content to it
  3. add corn starch and cooking oil
  4. Mix them together into one bowl
  5. on separate bowl, cut Carrots,ginger, garlic
  6. put the meat content on frying pan and continue stirring until meat turns brown
  7. On separate frying pan, add water. Add the content of the second bowl to the boiliing water. Simmer for 5 minutes
  8. Scoop out the contents from water and them to the meat
  9. Served chilled
